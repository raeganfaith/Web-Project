Among Us

for Novice and Experts

A website dedicated to provide gamers a safer and efficient gameplay experience in Among Us. This website contains information about the game's objectives, features, controls, maps, mods, tutorials, reviews, tips, and tricks.

What is Among Us?

Among Us is an online multiplayer game in which up to ten players can take on the roles of a 'Crewmate' or an 'Imposter.' To play the game, the Crewmates must identify the players in the game who are the Imposters.

Game Reviews

Game Features

Offering three different maps with different tasks for both roles as an Impostor and a crewmate, this game needs improvement.

How To Play

When the game begins, you will be assigned to one of the two roles: Crewmate or Imposter. If you're an Imposter, your mission is to kill all of the Crewmates without their noticing. If you're a Crewmate, your job is to fulfill all assigned tasks before the Imposter/s murder everyone.

Maps

Maps are a core mechanic of Among Us that make up the environment that players move in and interact with. The map played on is chosen before a game starts, with each one containing unique features, locations, and tasks.

Tips/Tricks

Among Us is a played by two roles, a crewmate, and an impostor. Winning as a crew member differs from winning as an Imposter. As a result, we've divided the tips and tricks into two categories so that you may gain an advantage no matter which you're playing as.
