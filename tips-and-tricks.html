Tips/Tricks

Learn from the Experts

Among Us Beginner's Guide in 4 Minutes - The Basics

Watch and Learn from the experts of the Among Us. This video is provided by the Clash Universe so that Novice and Experts can learn compilation of tips and tricks that will step up their competence in the gameplay.

Do's

#1
Do balanced tasks!

When you are playing with 10 people, it set more. When there are too few tasks, the Impostor has no chance of winning.

#2
Try to work with a partner!

Cooperation often allows for a quick win. It also boosts the speed of the progress of your game. Also, it is more fun to corner cremates if you have a cooperative partner!

#3
Accused!

When someone is accused, let them explain themselves. Perhaps he/she is being set up by Impostor. Make sure to observe all the loop holes you can determine in his/her alibi.

#4
Sabotage!

Using sabotage! Not only the lights are your sabotage. Remember about the reactor, communication and o2. It makes the progress of the crewmates slower. Giving you more opportunities and time for a killing spree!

#5
Have An Alibi!

Whenever a body is found and deliberations begin, everyone starts to say what they were doing and why they couldn't be the killer.

Don'ts

#1
Don’t be reckless!

Be careful where and when you kill. All it takes is a minute and you’ll be caught or charged with murder. Try to observe and watch the movements of the other cremates and set a perfect time to kill!

#2
Don’t tell who your partner is!

He still has a chance to win the game with your help, you should let your partner to enjoy and do his/her best to win your sides!

#3
No No Reason!

Don’t use Emergency Meeting for no reason! You waste your and other people’s time. Only do it when you have a reason.

#4
Don’t Leave!

If you’ve been killed or kicked, don’t leave the game! You can still finish your tasks, thus helping your team win. Also, don't just abandon your teammates!

#5
Stack Kills!

The biggest problem is that veteran players are waiting for the stack kill. People will bait the impostor by faking a stack and moving off of it to see who the killer is.
