Strategies

#1
Safety is number one!

with a person you trust means its much harder for an Impostor to kill you, but you may well be pairing upA with an Impostor yourself. Tread carefully.

#2
Try to Blend In

This is the most important part of being a believable "crewmate" as the Imposter! Pretend to do tasks (and let people see you) by standing by and waiting.

#3
Vent with Caution

Vents are a great way to get around, especially if you’ve trapped someone in a room, but if anyone sees you, they know you’re the Impostor.

#4
Take Breaks Between Tasks

Yeah, this contradicts the tips, but make sure you don’t have a laser focus on the tasks, otherwise you’ll never figure out the Impostor.

#5
J’accuse!

There is often limited time to discuss the Impostor after a body is discovered, so try and throw the suspicion elsewhere.
