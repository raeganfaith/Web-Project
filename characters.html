Best Mods You Should Try:

Sheriff Among Us mod

There’s a new sheriff in town, and he's wearing a spacesuit. The Among Us Sheriff mod lets one crewmember be the neighborhood regulation. The sheriff has a kill button, and can kill fakers without hanging tight for a group vote. In any case, there's just a single issue. Attempt to kill a guiltless player, and you'll pass on all things being equal.

Town of Impostors Among Us mod

You'll have a Impostor, and yet a Specialist who can resuscitate the dead and a Sheriff who can kill frauds. It's tomfoolery, however not excessively complicated, making for one of the cleanest mods on the web.

Skeld Among Us mod

This is definitely one of the best Among Us mods you can get your hands on, with tons of new game modes. For instance, the Identity Crisis mode makes everyone change their identity at every meeting. A Zombie mode makes one player try to infect all the others. There are 11 nutty game modes in all.

The Haunted Mansion Among Us mod

So spooky! This time you’re not on a spaceship. Instead, you’re in a haunted house complete with candlelight, cobwebs, hard-to-find light switches, and tombstones with actual skulls on them. This is kind of like living a mystery in a creepy movie, thanks to the high-quality worldbuilding of The Haunted Mansion Among Us mod.

Cursed Among Us mod

In this absolutely bonkers Among Us mod, some players are Cursed!. Those players have four tricky capacities. They can revile the crisis button to discharge players who use it, revile dead bodies and make them kill different players, revile assignments, and revile the vents so they'll eat Shams.

Crewbunny Among Us mod

Would Among Us be more fun if you played it with bunnies instead of spacemen? Who knows? You, that’s who, when you download and play the incredibly weird Crewbunny Among Us mod. There aren’t any special powers to explore with this mod. It just makes you look like you’ve got a carrot craving and a hop in your pants.

Vampy Among Us mod

This mod is super fun. The Impostors are vampires and will suck your blood dry. The thing is, you’ve all got little bat ears, so who’s the actual bloodsucker? You can use Bloodrush mode to spy on the crewmembers, kill, and even turn into a bat and fly between rooms. “I vant to vote you out!”

Town of Us Among Us mod

If you want a lot going on in your Among Us games, try the Town of Us mod. There are tons of roles (maybe too many?) to learn and try. There’s a Mayor, who can save up their votes and use them all at once, and the Lovers, who win the game if they both make the final three. Plus, you get a Sheriff, Engineer, Seer, Child, Spy, and 7 other roles with special powers.

Extra Roles Among Us mod

Very similar to the Town of Us Among Us mod, the Extra Roles mod has tons of additional roles, including the now-familiar Sheriff and Engineer roles, a Medic who can protect other players, and so many others. This mod’s huge selection of roles makes the gameplay equally chaotic and fun.

Love Couple Among Us Mod

From the Town of Us Among Us mod, “Sweethearts” win by being one of the last three players alive. Yet, be careful! On the off chance that one darling is killed, different bites the dust, as well. The darlings really assist the Fraud as they with doing combating to make due. But what if one lover is an Impostor? That’s when the real fun begins in the Love Couple Among Us mod.
