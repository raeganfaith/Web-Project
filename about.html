About Among Us

Among Us is an online multiplayer game in which up to ten players can take on the roles of a 'Crewmate' or an 'Imposter.' To play the game, the Crewmates must identify the players in the game who are the Imposters.

Game Information

Game Description

Among Us is a murder mystery game set aboard "The Skeld," the headquarters of the company MIRA, a research base on the planet Polus, or an Airship. One to three players will be chosen at random as Impostors, who must blend in and kill the Crewmates.

Game Objectives

The Crewmates' goal is to complete mini-games like fixing wiring, emptying garbage, and swiping an ID card while also figuring out who The Impostors are and voting them out.


The Impostors' goal is to kill off enough Crewmates to leave an equal number of Impostors and Crewmates, resulting in a win for The Impostors because they can't be voted out theoretically. They can do this thanks to the aforementioned sabotage mechanism, but their primary strategy is to kill each and every member one by one.
